Brand Awareness & Reach Campaigns

Before anyone buys from you, they need to know you exist. Our awareness campaigns use Meta's powerful reach and video view objectives to introduce your brand to highly relevant new audiences — building the recognition and trust that makes every other marketing channel work better. We target by interest, behavior, demographic, and lookalike modeling to ensure you're reaching people who genuinely match your ideal customer profile.

  • Audience research and persona development
  • Lookalike audience creation from your best customers
  • Video and carousel creative strategy for maximum reach
  • Brand lift measurement and recall tracking
  • Frequency management to prevent ad fatigue
  • Cross-placement optimization (Feed, Stories, Reels, Audience Network)

Lead Generation Campaigns

Meta's native Lead Ads format removes friction from the sign-up process — allowing users to submit their information without ever leaving the app. We design Lead Ad campaigns with compelling offers, optimized forms, and instant form follow-up sequences that capture and qualify leads while your cost per lead stays low.

  • Lead Ad form design with qualification questions
  • Offer creation and value proposition testing
  • CRM integration (HubSpot, Salesforce, Zapier) for instant lead delivery
  • Conversion campaign optimization for website lead forms
  • Lead quality scoring and validation
  • A/B testing of headlines, imagery, and CTAs

Retargeting & Dynamic Ads

The majority of your website visitors leave without converting. Our retargeting campaigns bring them back with personalized, timely ads that speak directly to where they are in the buying journey. For e-commerce clients, Dynamic Product Ads automatically show users the exact products they viewed — creating a highly personalized experience that dramatically improves conversion rates.

  • Meta Pixel setup and event configuration
  • Segmented retargeting by page visited, time on site, and actions taken
  • Dynamic Product Ad (DPA) catalog setup and optimization
  • Cart abandonment and checkout drop-off campaigns
  • Post-purchase upsell and cross-sell campaigns
  • Audience exclusions to prevent over-advertising to recent purchasers

Creative Strategy & Ad Creative Testing

In today's Meta environment, creative is the #1 performance lever. A great audience with weak creative will underperform. Our creative strategy team develops high-converting ad concepts — static images, carousels, video scripts, and UGC-style content — and runs systematic creative testing frameworks to rapidly identify what resonates and scale what works.

  • Creative concepting and ad copy development
  • Static image, carousel, and video ad creation
  • UGC-style creative briefs for authentic, high-converting content
  • Creative testing framework (DCO — Dynamic Creative Optimization)
  • Hook testing and first-3-second video analysis
  • Creative fatigue monitoring and systematic refresh cycles
