Common Questions

What You're Probably Wondering

How quickly will I hear back after submitting the form?

We guarantee a response within 24 business hours. Typically, you'll hear from us the same day. Once we connect, we schedule a discovery call at your convenience and deliver your free audit within 48 hours of that call.

What does the free audit include?

Depending on the service you're interested in, your audit covers: technical SEO issues and keyword opportunity analysis (for SEO), account structure, Quality Score, and wasted spend analysis (for Google Ads), or audience, creative, and pixel tracking review (for Meta Ads). All delivered in a clear, jargon-free report with prioritized recommendations.

Do you require long-term contracts?

No. We operate on 30-day rolling agreements. We believe in earning your business every month through results and communication — not through contractual lock-in. Read more about our approach on our About Us page.

What's your minimum monthly budget?

For paid media services (Google Ads and Meta Ads), we typically work with clients investing at least $2,000/month in ad spend plus management fees. For SEO, our engagements start at $1,500/month. We'll always recommend the right investment level for your goals — never more than is justified.
