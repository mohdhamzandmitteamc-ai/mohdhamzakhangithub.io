Search Campaign Management

Search campaigns are the backbone of most Google Ads strategies — placing your ads in front of users actively searching for what you sell. Our approach goes far beyond basic keyword bidding. We analyze search intent at a granular level, match keyword themes to tailored ad copy, and build negative keyword lists that prevent budget waste.

  • In-depth keyword research and intent mapping
  • Ad copy creation with multi-variant A/B testing
  • Smart Bidding strategy implementation (tCPA, tROAS, Maximize Conversions)
  • Granular negative keyword management
  • Ad extension optimization (sitelinks, callouts, structured snippets)
  • Quality Score improvement and landing page alignment
  • Search term report analysis and query mining

Google Shopping & Performance Max

For e-commerce businesses, Shopping campaigns are non-negotiable. We optimize your Google Merchant Center feed, structure campaigns by product category and margin, and leverage Performance Max to find incremental conversions across all of Google's channels — Search, Display, YouTube, Gmail, and Maps — from a single campaign.

  • Google Merchant Center setup and feed optimization
  • Shopping campaign architecture by category, margin, and performance
  • Performance Max campaign creation and asset group strategy
  • Product title and description optimization for shopping queries
  • Price competitiveness monitoring and bid adjustments
  • Custom labels for promotional and high-margin product targeting

Display & Remarketing Campaigns

Most website visitors don't convert on their first visit. Our remarketing campaigns keep your brand visible across 2 million+ sites in Google's Display Network, re-engaging warm audiences with tailored messages that guide them back to convert. We also use Display for prospecting — reaching new audiences based on intent signals, demographics, and affinity.

  • Standard and dynamic remarketing campaign setup
  • Customer match and similar audiences targeting
  • Responsive Display Ad (RDA) creation and testing
  • In-market and affinity audience targeting for prospecting
  • Frequency capping and placement exclusions for brand safety
  • Cross-campaign audience segmentation (1-day, 7-day, 30-day visitors)

YouTube Advertising

Video advertising builds brand trust at scale — and our YouTube campaigns are designed to stop the scroll, communicate your value proposition clearly, and drive action. We handle creative strategy, targeting, and performance optimization end-to-end.

  • YouTube campaign strategy (TrueView In-Stream, Bumpers, Discovery)
  • Audience targeting: custom intent, remarketing, demographic layers
  • Creative brief development and production collaboration
  • View-through conversion tracking and attribution
  • Placement exclusions and brand safety controls
